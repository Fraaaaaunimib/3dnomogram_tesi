Human - AI Interaction Assessment

Does AI help or hinder human decisions?

This online tool supports the evaluation of Human-AI interaction in binary classification tasks by analyzing how humans rely on AI predictions and the impact of that reliance.
By uploading your decision data, the tool generates benefit and reliance diagrams and scores, helping you understand when and how AI contributes to better decision-making. It is particularly useful for studying trust calibration, decision support systems (DSS), and optimizing the design of human-centered AI.
